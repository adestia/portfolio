Snake Bites

There are two main types of snake bites. Remember to treat all bites as emergencies if you aren't sure!

Dry bites

No venom is released

Venomous Bites

Venom is released

Treatment

  1. Call 000 and ask for an ambulance.
  2. Apply a large pressure bandage over the bite area as soon as possible. If you don't have any, use any cloth you have - but make sure it's firm and tight!
  3. Keep applying layers around the bite and extending from the bite area. Cover as much of the area as possible. We want to make sure the person doesnt move and cause the venom to spread.
  4. Using a stick, or a special medical stick called a splint, to keep the limb straight.
  5. Wait for the ambulance to arrive.

Did You Know?

Most snake bites only happen if you try to kill or catch them. If you see a snake, go the other way!

DONT!

  • Cut the bitten area
  • Try to suck out the venom
  • Wash the affected area
