Bee Stings

For normal bee stings that don't cause an allergic reaction, you can treat them at home!

Here's how you do it:

Treatment

Oh no! Someone's gotten stung by a bee, follow the steps to heal them.

Step 1

Remove the stinger from the area by sliding out the stinger using your fingernails or nails. Don't pull it!

Try to slide the stinger out of the finger and into the box.

Good job!

Step 2

Wash the area with water. Try drag the water onto the affected area!

Nice and easy.

Step 3

Apply ice or a cold-pack to soothe and reduce swelling.

Awesome, you've got this!

Step 4

If the person is allergic, apply pressure to the bite. Try clicking the hurt finger below to apply pressure!

Yay!

Step 5

Call 000!
